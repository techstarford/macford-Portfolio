Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining the community

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual payments.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime with no hidden fees. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 7-day free trial for the Starter plan so you can experience the community before committing.

How often are new tutorials added?

New tutorials are added weekly, with exclusive content for Pro and Mastermind members updated twice weekly.

What if I need help with a specific project?

Pro and Mastermind members get direct support through our private channels. Starter members can ask questions in our monthly Q&A.
