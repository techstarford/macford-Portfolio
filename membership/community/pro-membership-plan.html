Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Pro membership

How often are the live coding sessions?

We host live coding sessions every week, typically on Wednesdays at 7 PM EST. All sessions are recorded and available to members who can't attend live.

Can I cancel my Pro membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime with no penalty. Your membership will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

What programming languages are covered?

We primarily focus on JavaScript (React, Node.js), Python, and modern web development. However, the principles taught apply to any language, and we often cover language-agnostic topics like system design and algorithms.

How do the code reviews work?

Each month, you can submit up to 200 lines of code for review. Our team will provide detailed feedback on style, architecture, performance, and best practices within 72 hours.

Is there a free trial for the Pro membership?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first week, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.
