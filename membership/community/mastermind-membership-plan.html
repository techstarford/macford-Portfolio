Mastermind FAQ

Answers to common questions about our VIP program

How does the application process work?

After submitting your application, we'll schedule a consultation call to discuss your goals and ensure the program is a good fit. Acceptance is based on availability and alignment with your objectives.

What's the time commitment?

Members typically spend 5-10 hours per week on mentorship sessions, project work, and skill development. The program is designed to work alongside full-time jobs or studies.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, we offer one 30-day pause per year. Just contact us at least 7 days before your next billing date.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the program, we'll provide a full refund.
